# M1 Status — 24-Month Vertical Slice

## Completed

- Three regional associations with different population, football participation, execution capacity, integrity and development conditions.
- Six professional clubs with cash, debt, revenue, wages, academy quality, owners, integrity and supporter bases.
- Twenty-five-player first-team rosters with positions, age, ability, potential, fitness, morale, injuries, wages, contracts, nationality and homegrown status.
- Two complete double round-robin domestic seasons with league tables, form, xG, possession, attendance, gate receipts, prize money and forfeits.
- A complete six-team World Cup qualifying group: ten rounds and all thirty fixtures.
- Dynamic national-team strength and fan-trust reactions to expected versus actual results.
- Coach-education policy with funding limits, regional leakage, completion rates and nine-month delivery delays.
- Youth-match funding with capacity limits, regional implementation and delayed participation effects.
- Cross-ministry school-football agreement requiring political leverage and cofunding.
- Club licensing reform producing compliance, restructuring, sanctions or rule-gaming.
- Monthly club financial settlement, wage arrears and exclusion after persistent failure.
- Three presidential strategies and a custom interactive budget.
- A polished Streamlit command centre with monthly controls, six working views and full audit history.
- Monthly history, six-month dashboards and a month-24 board confidence review.
- Fifteen deterministic tests covering policy delay, regional execution, budgets, club finances, rosters, leagues, international qualification and strategy outcomes.

## Not yet complete

- Annual treasury replenishment and a second budget cycle.
- New policy and personnel decisions during the term rather than only at inauguration.
- Political events, media crises, injuries with named narratives and club-owner bargaining.
- Continental club competitions, promotion/relegation and a live transfer market.

M1 remains open until the second budget cycle and mid-term decision system are implemented. The competition and visual layers now exceed the original M1 scope.
