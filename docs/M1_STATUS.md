# M1 Status — 24-Month Vertical Slice ✅

M1 is complete on the `year-two-governance` milestone branch and is ready to merge after CI.

## Governance and finance

- Three regional associations with different population, football participation, execution capacity, integrity and development conditions.
- An opening presidential budget plus a second-year funding cycle.
- Year-two income generated from political capital, league commercial health, World Cup qualifying performance and integrity reputation.
- Six mandatory presidential decisions in months 4, 6, 8, 12, 16 and 20.
- Decision consequences that alter money, political capital, trust, integrity, regional capacity, medical standards, club finances and national-team strength.
- Interactive time blocking: the web campaign stops until the president signs a decision.
- Cross-ministry school-football agreements, coach education, youth-match funding and licensing reform.
- A month-24 board confidence review with an explainable score.

## Clubs, players and competitions

- Six professional clubs with cash, debt, revenue, wages, academy quality, owners, integrity and supporter bases.
- Twenty-five-player opening rosters with position, age, ability, potential, fitness, morale, injuries, wages, contracts, nationality and homegrown status.
- Two complete double round-robin domestic seasons with tables, form, xG, possession, attendance, gate receipts, prize money and forfeits.
- A complete six-team World Cup qualifying group: ten rounds and all thirty fixtures.
- Dynamic national-team strength and fan-trust reactions to expected versus actual results.
- Monthly club financial settlement, wage arrears and exclusion after persistent failure.
- Two deterministic transfer windows with homegrown, open-market and financial-control policies.
- Transfer fees moving real club cash, player objects changing rosters and new wages changing the buyer's monthly cost base.

## Interface and verification

- A polished Streamlit command centre with seven working views.
- Monthly and three-month controls plus automatic stopping at the next cabinet decision.
- Cabinet, annual-finance and transfer-market records.
- Monthly history, six-month snapshots and a full policy/match audit log.
- Command-line reports for decisions, finance, transfers, qualifying and the domestic table.
- GitHub Actions regression testing, including downloadable pytest failure logs.
