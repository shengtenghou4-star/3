# Political Careers, Patronage and Justice

Version 0.12 turns organizational blocs into named people with careers, relationships and legal exposure.

## Named career pool

The long-term history contains persistent people from:

- national sports, finance and education authorities;
- provincial football associations;
- the professional league and club-owner council;
- broadcasters and commercial sponsors;
- the players' union and supporters' federation;
- the national football association cabinet.

Every person has age, competence, integrity, ambition, loyalty, network power, legal exposure and a chronological career. Cabinet officials and presidential candidates are selected from this pool. A regional executive can therefore enter the national cabinet, serve as caretaker chair and later run for president.

## Patronage network

Relationships can be family, mentor, alumni, professional, commercial, campaign or patronage ties. Each tie has strength, disclosure status and lifecycle state.

A relationship is not itself misconduct. Risk emerges when:

```text
strong relationship
+ non-disclosure
+ control over appointments, contracts or licensing
+ low personal integrity
= higher legal exposure
```

Independent investigations disclose related ties. Final sanctions weaken or dissolve implicated networks.

## Referral choices

When credible material reaches the president, the president can choose only the referral route:

1. independent referral and public disclosure;
2. internal disciplinary review;
3. political suppression and protection of the network.

The president cannot choose guilt or innocence.

## Formal case pipeline

Cases can move through:

```text
referral
-> investigation or internal review
-> charging decision
-> first-instance trial
-> appeal
-> final outcome
```

Evidence growth depends on prosecutor capacity, case independence, disclosed relationships and network interference. A trial can acquit. An appeal can uphold, reduce or reverse a first-instance conviction.

A final ban removes the person from cabinet and presidential candidate pools. A reversal restores eligibility but does not erase disclosed relationships or political memory.

## Suppression risk

Suppressed material does not vanish. Hidden network strength, personal exposure and weak national integrity determine whether it leaks. A leaked suppressed case reopens with stronger independence and an additional political cost because the original interference becomes part of the scandal.

## Career continuity

Presidential resignation, caretaker accession, snap-election victory and scheduled succession all write real career postings. Outgoing presidents become former presidents rather than remaining incorrectly marked as incumbents.

Annual career cycles age people, develop competence, expand networks, promote ambitious actors and retire older non-officeholders. The same people persist across the twenty-year football history.

## Deterministic saves

JSON replay covers:

- people and complete career postings;
- patronage ties and disclosure state;
- justice cases and procedural stages;
- evidence, independence, outcomes and appeals;
- cabinet appointments and presidential transitions;
- all existing football, club, player, election and coalition state.

A fingerprint mismatch rejects the save rather than silently accepting a divergent political history.
