# Player Perspective Constitution

Football Republic is a football-association president simulator. The player controls one named president, not the entire political system.

## Player identity

At the beginning of a career, one president ID is permanently assigned to the player.

The player may:

- sign national football budgets and programs;
- appoint and dismiss national-association officials;
- negotiate with ministries, regional associations, clubs, players and sponsors;
- decide referral routes for credible investigations;
- choose national-team, competition, licensing and development strategy;
- seek renewal and defend the governing coalition.

The player may not directly control:

- club transfers, contracts or match selections;
- stakeholder votes;
- judicial charging, verdicts or appeals;
- successor candidates or their coalition bargains;
- sponsor withdrawal, owner investment or player decisions.

## Leaving office

Resignation, removal, electoral defeat or the consecutive-term limit ends the playable political career immediately.

The persistent football world does not end. The player can either:

1. finish the career and read the legacy report; or
2. enter observer mode and watch successor governments continue the same clubs, players, debts, laws, relationships and competitions.

Observer mode never restores decision authority.

## Information boundaries

The simulation continues to store exact NPC competence, integrity, loyalty, ambition, network power, hidden relationships and legal evidence. These values are not shown directly to the player.

The president receives information through:

- cabinet performance reviews;
- public declarations and official votes;
- licensing and financial reports;
- disclosed relationships;
- audit findings;
- investigation, trial and appeal notices;
- political estimates from the secretary-general;
- rumors only when a source and confidence level exist.

A relationship hidden from the president remains hidden in the player interface until disclosure, audit or investigation reveals it.

## Career ending report

When the player leaves office, the game freezes a personal legacy report containing:

- tenure length and terms served;
- exit reason and successor;
- football and political scores;
- promise fulfillment;
- national-team, league, integrity and supporter outcomes;
- trophies during the player's tenure;
- achievements, failures and final historical verdict.

Later observer simulation cannot rewrite this report.

## Save integrity

President-career saves contain both:

- the complete deterministic world save; and
- the fixed player ID, career status, exit month and legacy report.

A fingerprint mismatch rejects the save. Loading a save may never change which president the player was controlling.
